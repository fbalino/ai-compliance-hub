## Overview

An **impact assessment** (sometimes called an algorithmic impact assessment or AIA) is a structured review of how an AI system may affect people, particularly in high-stakes decision-making contexts.

### Under the Colorado AI Act

The Colorado AI Act requires deployers of high-risk AI systems to conduct impact assessments that evaluate:
- The system's known risks of algorithmic discrimination
- The deployer's policies for managing those risks
- The data used to train and test the system
- Metrics used to evaluate system performance

Assessments must be completed before deployment and updated annually or whenever there is a material change to the system.
