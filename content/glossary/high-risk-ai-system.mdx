## Definitions by Law

The term "high-risk AI system" is defined differently across key regulations:

### Colorado AI Act

Under Colorado SB 24-205, a high-risk AI system is any AI that makes, or is a substantial factor in making, a **consequential decision** affecting Colorado consumers in areas including:
- Employment (hiring, promotion, termination, pay)
- Education (enrollment, financial aid)
- Financial services (credit, lending, insurance)
- Healthcare (diagnosis, treatment, coverage)
- Housing (rental, mortgage)
- Legal services

### EU AI Act

The EU AI Act uses a broader definition based on application domain. High-risk AI includes systems used in:
- Critical infrastructure
- Education and vocational training
- Employment and workers management
- Essential private and public services
- Law enforcement
- Migration and border control
- Administration of justice
