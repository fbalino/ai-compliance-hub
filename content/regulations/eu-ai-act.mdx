## Overview

The **EU Artificial Intelligence Act** (Regulation 2024/1689) is the world's first comprehensive legal framework for AI, establishing a risk-based approach to AI governance across the European Union.

## Risk Classification

The Act divides AI systems into four risk tiers:

1. **Unacceptable risk** — banned outright (social scoring, real-time biometric surveillance)
2. **High risk** — extensive requirements before market placement
3. **Limited risk** — transparency obligations only
4. **Minimal risk** — no specific requirements
